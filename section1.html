History
The English Premier League (EPL) was founded in 1991.

In the 1990s, English First Division(football league before EPL) clubs believed that in order for football in England to develop and flourish, they would need to reconstruct the entire league.

In 1992, 22 teams resigned from the First Division league to sign with the newly crafted Premier League

The league began and struck their first television deal with sky sports, who helped develop the league into what it is today.
